## Release Calendar Pattern (Typical Year)
1. **Turbo Attax** - Around Silverstone/Austrian GP (mid-season, ~July)
2. **Topps Now** - Ongoing after each race weekend (limited time)
3. **Lights Out** - Mid-season (UK exclusive, ~Aug)
4. **Dynasty** - November (before Christmas)
5. **Chrome** - Late November to January (main release)
6. **Sapphire** - 1-2 months after Chrome
7. **LogoFractor** - Around same time or shortly after Chrome
8. **Finest** - Spring (but rumored cancelled for 2025)
9. **Paddock Pass** - Before Christmas (but rumored cancelled for 2025)

- Returning inserts: Helmet Collection, Speed Demons, Futuro, The Grid
